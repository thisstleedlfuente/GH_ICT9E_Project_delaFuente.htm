"Accidents happen when you least expect it." There are many ways to be able to prevent danger (fire) to ruin your worked for home. These ways may also help you and your family to keep safe and live a happy life.

Don't forget to remember the routine when there's a fire and always check before going to sleep or going out.


How to Prevent Fire
Unplug Items that are not in use Items like hair irons, irons, hair curlers, chargers, rice cooker, or other random wires should be unplugged after use. The energy is flowing in it may cause fire to flammable items.
Avoid Plugging Several Devices Plugging too much may cause short circuits and it may suddenly explode without you knowing. It may also cause fire and burn wires and gadgets that are connected.
Do Not Charge At Night Before you sleep, don't plug in you're charger. You may not know when your charger will explode and end up causing fire to your house while you are asleep.
Don't Leave Candles Unattended Candles may accidentally spread to flammable items, like curtains, paper, etc., which may lead to fire inside the house.
